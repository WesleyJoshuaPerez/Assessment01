Troy Francis N. Mendoza

My name is Troy Francis Navales Mendoza. I was born in September 9, 2004 at Olongapo City, Zambales
and currently 20 years of age. I am currently at my third year of college majoring in Information Technology
specializing on Network and Web Application. From my chosen field of study, I excel on front-end development,
graphics design and networking. My interests includes watching movies, reading books, designing digital posters
and I am also pretty good at writing critiques, essays and screenplays.

My family bought a land, built a house and relocated from Zambales to Bulacan when I was growing up due to my
father's diligence and hard work abroad. My mother is named Jimmelyn Mendoza and she was born and bred in Olongapo City as well.
She is currently handling a business in our home. My father is named Jayson Mendoza and he was born and bred in Bulacan.
He has 2 brothers and 7 sisters. He is currently working as a cashier in Toronto, Canada. I have one sister named Chloe
and she is currently studying at a high school back in my hometown.

When I was born, I was diagnosed with a heart condition which I fortunately grew out of as I enter toddlerhood.
During my elementary days, I am usually asthmatic—partly due to my heart condition—so I was barred from engaging
in rigorous physical activities like playing with my schoolmates after school. But at this time, I discovered my
passion for books and writing so even though I do not have a lot friends, I usually pass time by reading and my
teachers started noticing my proficiency in the English language so they started sending me to journalism competitions.
I won first place at a feature writing contest when I was in sixth grade and my mother has never been so proud of me.

I studied at a national high school in Bulacan and I am a consistent honor student. During my senior high school,
the COVID-19 pandemic happened forcing me, my mother and my sister to temporarily move to Bataan in search for a
job so I graduated high school altogether here in Orion, Bataan back in July 2022. I was originally going to pursue
a writing major but they do not offer anything relative to it here in BPSU so I took Information Technology instead.
On my freshman year, I had a hard time adjusting to the environment because college is so far different from high
school but I ended up knowing and befriending a lot of interesting, talented and nice people. But in 2023,
in my second year of college, my mother and sister had to go back in Bulacan because my father got a job offer
in Toronto, Canada. I had to stay in Bataan and live with my uncle's house until I finish college.

Back in 2020, I started watching a lot of movies because we're all stuck at home and I don't really have anything much going on.
I completely fell in love with the art of filmmaking so it is a dream of mine to visit Hollywood until I have saved enough.
My favorite filmmakers includes Éric Rohmer, Charlie Kaufman, Martin Scorsese, Gregg Araki, Luca Guadagnino, Hayao Miyazaki
and David Fincher. Fincher's 2010 biopic called "The Social Network" is very influential to me and is the film that made me
consider taking a computer-related course in college because I thought making websites and programming was cool.
